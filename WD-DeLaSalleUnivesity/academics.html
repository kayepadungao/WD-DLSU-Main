Academic Programs

De La Salle University (DLSU) offers a diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, research, and innovation, the university provides students with a dynamic and holistic education that prepares them for leadership roles in their respective fields. DLSU’s academic programs are housed under several colleges and schools, including: Br. Andrew Gonzalez College of Education – Focused on training future educators and academic leaders. College of Computer Studies – A hub for technology, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity programs. Gokongwei College of Engineering – One of the top engineering schools in the Philippines, known for producing skilled engineers and innovators. Ramon V. del Rosario College of Business – Offers world-class business education, emphasizing entrepreneurship and ethical leadership. College of Science – Home to programs in biology, chemistry, physics, and other natural sciences. College of Liberal Arts – Provides a strong foundation in the humanities, social sciences, and communication. School of Economics – Develops future economists, policy-makers, and financial experts. College of Law – Trains legal professionals with a focus on justice, ethics, and service to society. DLSU continuously updates its curricula to align with global standards and industry demands, ensuring that students graduate with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills. The university also fosters a culture of research and innovation, encouraging students and faculty to contribute to advancements in science, technology, business, and social development. With strong international partnerships, DLSU provides students with exchange programs, dual-degree opportunities, and global exposure, reinforcing its mission of producing competent and socially responsible professionals.
