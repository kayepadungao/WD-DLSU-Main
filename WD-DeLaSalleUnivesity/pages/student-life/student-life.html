Experience Student Life at DLSU

Vibrant Campus Culture

De La Salle University offers a dynamic and engaging student life with numerous opportunities for personal and professional growth. The university hosts a variety of events, from academic symposia to cultural festivals, making every day an exciting experience.

Student Organizations

With over 100 student organizations, DLSU provides students with opportunities to pursue their interests in academics, arts, sports, and social advocacy. These organizations foster leadership and camaraderie among Lasallians.

Sports and Recreation

As home to the Green Archers, DLSU has a strong sports culture. The university offers facilities for basketball, volleyball, swimming, and more. Students can also join intramural leagues and fitness programs to stay active.

Community Engagement

Lasallians are deeply involved in outreach and social development programs. Through the Center for Social Concern and Action (COSCA), students participate in initiatives that serve marginalized communities and promote sustainable development.
